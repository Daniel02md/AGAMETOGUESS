A GAME TO GUESS - STEP BY STEP

This game consists in guessing a random number sorted by a software.

  1. 1. Inside the text box, type your guess to the random number

  2. 2. Click in the button "GUESS NOW!" to check if you typed the right number.

  3. 3. Down the text box, there will be tips about the sorted number, based on your guess.

  4. 4. Do the previous steps over and over again, until appears the message, "YOU WON!".

PLAY HERE!

The guesses should be between 1 and 100
